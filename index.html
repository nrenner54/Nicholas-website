Welcome Home!

My goal is simple. I want to show YOU, that practice makes perfect, and I figured I would do so by providing examples of my models made in Blender. I'll be showcasing the different levels of progress in my modeling journey by presenting my firearm models made from long ago, to present day.


About me

At the time of writing this, I turned 19 this month, and I currently live in Hawaii and study at UH Hilo campus. This website is a project for a coding class, and since I already have a portfolio, I figured having this little positive website would be nice. I used to experiment with Video editing, and you can see an example of my work below.


Even as I struggle with coding and get frustrated, I remember how I used to struggle and get frustrated with modeling and video editing. I can now look from where I am and realize that if I just practice and learn more code, I can become a better coder overall.
